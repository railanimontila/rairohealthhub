Health

-is the state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease and infirmity.

-it is also the condition of well-being

Here is the list of aspects of health:

    Physical health-refers to the state of the body that includes its composition, function, maintenance, development.
  1. Mental health -it refers to the cognitive ability of a person to improve the quality of his life.
  2. Social health -refers to one's ability to build and relate and maintain relationships with his/her family and community.
  3. Moral health -refers to one's belifs, faith, and values.
  4. Emotional health -refers to the ability of a person to accept the feelings of his/her own and that of others.
