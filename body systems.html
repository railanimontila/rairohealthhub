Body sytems

It is a set of organs working together to keep you healthy.These body systems maake up the human body as awhole.Examples are the immune system, reproductive system, and etc.

Here is the list of the following body systems.

  1. Skeletal system-it protects and supports vital organs and consist of skull, ribs, and limbs.
  2. Immune system-it consists of white blood cells and antibodies and it is in charge of defending the human body.
  3. Reproductive system-it coordinates activities related to procreation and reproduction and it consists of gonads and sex cells.
  4. Lymphatic system-it cleanse the fluids knwn as lymph and aides the immune system in fighting diseases.
  5. Respiratory system- it coordinates exchange of gases of oxygen and cabon dioxide and supplies oxygen to the body.
  6. Digestive system-it consists of the stomach and the intestines and its job is to supply nutrients for use
    7. Cardiovascular system-it consists of the heart and blood vessels and its job is to pump and deliver blood to the human body
